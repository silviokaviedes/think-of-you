## App Details

App name: Thinking of You

Short description:
Stay quietly connected with the people who matter most.

Full description:
Thinking of You is a private connection app for sending simple, non-verbal "thinking of you" moments to the people you care about.

Create an account, connect with trusted partners, and send a small signal when someone is on your mind. Add an optional mood, see recent sent and received moments, and use the event log and statistics to understand your connection over time.

Core features:
- Send "thinking of you" moments to connected people.
- Add optional mood context such as love, happy, calm, worried, grateful, or neutral.
- Manage connection requests and support more than one connection.
- See sent and received counts or the most recent event on your dashboard.
- Review recent history in the event log.
- View mood statistics for each connection.
- Receive real-time updates while the app is open.
- Receive push notifications when configured.
- Delete your account from the Profile screen.

The app does not include ads or third-party analytics in the current implementation.
