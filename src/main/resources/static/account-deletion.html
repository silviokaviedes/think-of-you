Thinking of You Account Deletion

This page explains how to delete your Thinking of You account and associated data.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Log in to your Thinking of You account.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. Go to the Delete account section.
  4. Enter your current password and type your username exactly.
  5. Confirm the permanent deletion prompt.

The current implementation deletes your user account, connections, thought history, refresh tokens, and stored push tokens.

If you cannot access the app

Contact the app operator using the public privacy/support contact listed in the published privacy policy and request account deletion.
