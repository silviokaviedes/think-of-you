Thinking of You Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 27, 2026

These standards describe how Thinking of You prohibits and addresses child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Thinking of You is not designed for children, but child safety rules apply to all use of the app.

Developer and Contact

Developer/app name: Thinking of You

Child safety contact: thinkingofyou@kaviedes.de

Website or support page: https://think-of-you-production.up.railway.app/

Zero Tolerance Standard

Thinking of You strictly prohibits any content, conduct, username, profile information, connection activity, or attempted use of the service that facilitates, promotes, depicts, requests, shares, or normalizes CSAE or CSAM.

Prohibited activity includes, but is not limited to:

  • Sexualization, exploitation, abuse, grooming, trafficking, sextortion, or endangerment of a minor.
  • Requests for, offers of, links to, or distribution of CSAM.
  • Using the app to contact, identify, coerce, manipulate, or exploit a minor.
  • Any attempt to evade review, reporting, account action, or law-enforcement processes related to child safety.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users and third parties can report child safety concerns, suspected CSAE, or suspected CSAM by contacting: thinkingofyou@kaviedes.de.

If a child is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement or the appropriate child protection authority first.

Review and Enforcement

When Thinking of You receives a child safety report or otherwise obtains actual knowledge of suspected CSAE or CSAM, the app operator may take appropriate action, including:

  • Reviewing the reported account, username, connection, or related app activity.
  • Removing prohibited content where the app can store or display it.
  • Suspending or deleting accounts involved in prohibited conduct.
  • Preserving relevant records where legally appropriate.
  • Reporting confirmed CSAM or imminent child safety threats to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC), law enforcement, or the relevant regional authority as required or permitted by law.

App Design and Current Capabilities

Thinking of You is a private connection app. The current implementation does not provide public feeds, media uploads, image sharing, video sharing, or open chat rooms. User-controlled content is limited, but these child safety standards still apply to all account names, connection behavior, reports, and use of the service.

Cooperation and Compliance

Thinking of You aims to comply with applicable child safety laws and Google Play child safety requirements. The designated child safety contact can receive notices from Google Play and other appropriate parties about suspected CSAE or CSAM involving the app.
